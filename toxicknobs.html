Toxic Knobs - Packaging Design

Toxic Knobs, an independent gear knob manufacturer, sought eco-friendly packaging for their handcrafted recycled gear knobs made from ground and reformed plastic bottles.

Find out more

Speaking with the client and identifying their needs, we incorporated into the design a product description section to be filled in by hand. This idea was to reduce waste from having pre-determined packaging that could only be used for a specific model of gear knob. This enables Toxic Knobs to have limitless options and production run sizes, which, due to the nature of demand and material availability, is crucial.
