Magick Makers - Packaging Design

Organic natural skincare and perfume business, Magick Makers required a bottle and packaging design for their new fragrance “Enchanted Forest”

Drawing from the brand’s focus on organic ingredients and its witchcraft-inspired aesthetic, we developed a packaging design that highlights its unique identity. The bottle design took cues from old-world elixirs, evolving into a distinctive cauldron shape. This form not only reflects the brand’s themes but also evokes the artistry of potion-making, blending raw, carefully selected ingredients to create "magick."

The packaging was designed to complement the bottle, staying true to the brand guidelines with carefully chosen colors, typography, and textures. Embossed foil logos and a textured print bring together the magic and the rawness of the ingredients. The final design was created to stand out and resonate with Magick Makers’ audience, combining creativity and practicality in every detail.
